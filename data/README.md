# Data Folder – Milestone 2

This folder contains datasets used to analyze labour market outcomes by field of study (CIP) in Ontario.

## 1. Labour Force Status by Field of Study (CIP).csv
Source: Statistics Canada (Census table 98-10-0401-01)  
Description: Labour force status by major field of study (CIP) and geography.  
Geography filter used: Ontario  
Purpose: Compare employment/unemployment outcomes across fields of study.

## 2. Earnings by Field of Study (Income).csv
Source: Statistics Canada (Census table 98-10-0409-01)  
Description: Employment income by major field of study (CIP), geography, and education level.  
Geography filter used: Ontario  
Purpose: Compare income outcomes across fields of study.

## 3. Median Earnings of Postsecondary Graduates (Post-Grad Outcomes).csv
Source: Statistics Canada (table 37-10-0284-01)  
Description: Median employment income of postsecondary graduates two years after graduation, by field of study groupings.  
Geography filter used: Ontario  
Purpose: Early-career earnings comparison across study areas.
